To run this game you need to install/have installed:
- python
- pygame
- numpy
Things to note:
- On the board 0 to 9 are pawns, 10 to 19 are knights, 20 to 29 are bishops, 30 to 39 are rooks, 40 to 49 are queens and 50 to 59 are kings.
- The vertical dimension is currently labeled x and to vertical dimension is labeled y, for reasons unknown.
