You can move pieces or pawns by typing the number assigned to the piece or pawn you wanna move, 
the rank you want to move it to (0 to 7), and the file you want to move it to (0 to 7). 0 to 9 are pawns, 10 to 19 are knights,
20 to 29 are bishops, 30 to 39 are rooks, 40 to 49 are queens and 50 to 59 are kings.
For example:
1
3
0
Please note that the vertical dimension is currently labeled x and to vertical dimension is labeled y, for reasons unknown.
